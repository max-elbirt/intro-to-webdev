My Favourite Book

Iron Gustav

"Iron Gustav" tells the story of a german patriarch and his family before, during and after the first world war. The war plunges his life and family into chaos, and changes everything Gustav knew about his country, and his understanding of happiness, parenthood and true misery and hardship.

why i like this book

I like "Iron Gustav" because of its overall anti-war message, conveying to the reader the terrible hardships of war on the battlefield and at home.

a quote from the book

A quote from the book: You only want to tyrannise, you’re only happy when we’re all trembling before you. You’re just like your Kaiser. He who doesn’t obey is shot down…




A place I Enjoyed Visiting

Zichron Yaakov

Zichron Yaakov is one of the first modern settlements in Israel. The town was founded in 1882, and in the present day is known for its views, fresh air and vineyards.

why i like this place

As someone who was raised in Tel Aviv (a comparatively major city) I enjoyed the feeling of a small town atop a mountain and somewhat removed from the rest of the world.
